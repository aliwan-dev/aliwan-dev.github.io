Sudoku Lite is a simple and lightweight Sudoku game designed for players who enjoy quick and relaxing puzzle sessions.

Focus on solving puzzles without distractions. Perfect for beginners or anyone who wants a straightforward and stress-free experience.

### Features:
- **Easy and Medium Levels** — Ideal for new players or casual puzzle lovers.
- **Simple and Clean UI** — Enjoy a smooth and clutter-free playing experience.
- **Lightweight and Fast** — Perfect for quick play anytime, anywhere.

More features, levels, and improvements will be added as the app continues to grow. Start your Sudoku journey with this simple and enjoyable lite version!
