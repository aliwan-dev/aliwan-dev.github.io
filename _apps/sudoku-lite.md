Sudoku (lite) brings the classic puzzle experience to your mobile device without the bloat. Focus on the logic and the numbers with a clean interface that helps you concentrate on solving the grid.

### Key Features
- **Clean and lightweight** app size for easy installation and performance.
- **Beginner to advanced levels** to challenge your skills.
- **Smart hints** and error highlighting to help you solve.
- **Dark mode support** for better night-time play.
