QR Code Scanner by Aliwan Dev is designed for users who value speed and privacy. It allows you to scan any QR code or barcode instantly, providing relevant actions based on the content (URLs, text, contacts, etc.).

### Key Features
- **Instant scanning** with auto-focus for quick results.
- **Scan history** to keep track of your activity locally.
- **Lightweight design** that ensures minimal battery usage.
- **Privacy-focused** with all processing staying on your device.
