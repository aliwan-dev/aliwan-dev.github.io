QR Code Scanner & Barcode Reader — fast, free, and built for everyday use. 

Whether you're scanning product barcodes at the store, checking a WiFi QR code, saving a contact from a business card, or verifying a URL before you open it — this app handles it all in one tap.

### Key Features:
- **Instant Scanning** — Detects QR codes and all major barcode formats (EAN-13, Code 128, Data Matrix, PDF417, and more) in a split second.
- **Custom QR Generation** — Create your own QR codes for links, text, WiFi, contacts, or calendar events.
- **Scan History** — Never lose a scan. All scanning activities are saved with date and time for easy retrieval.
- **Flashlight Support** — Easily scan in low-light environments with the built-in flash toggle.
- **Batch Scanning** — Scan multiple codes in sequence and review them all at once.
- **Safe & Private** — Only requires camera permission. We don’t track your personal data.

Designed to be lightweight and simple, QR Code Scanner: Barcode Reader is the only tool you need to decode the world around you.
