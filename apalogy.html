My Apology and Final Message to the App HIVE Community

A sincere explanation and request from Aliwan-dev regarding the recent daily task feedback issue.

Author: Aliwan-dev
App: QR Code Scanner
Context: Daily task / feedback

Dear App HIVE Team and Community Members,

I apologize if using this feature to express my disappointment causes any inconvenience to anyone.

If today I am removed from Session 1 of the App HIVE community, I want to clarify my situation and share my thoughts sincerely.

I admit that I made a mistake by submitting the wrong screenshot for my daily task feedback. However, as soon as I received the rejection, I immediately took a new and correct screenshot and resubmitted it at 19:27 local time.

This means there was still about 4 hours and 33 minutes remaining before the daily task reset. Considering my time zone (UTC+7), that would be a total of 11 hours and 33 minutes for my resubmitted feedback to receive a response.

Unfortunately, until the daily task reset, I did not receive any response—neither approval nor rejection. Later, I found that my feedback was actually approved after the submission deadline, at 07:52 local time (UTC 01:52), which was beyond the system’s time limit.

Given these facts, I feel it is not entirely fair that I received a penalty, as I have always completed my daily tasks consistently and on time.

Every action has its consequences, and I hold no anger toward the app owner who did not respond in time. However, I do feel disappointed by how the situation was handled.

If I am removed from this group, I will still honor my commitment to keep the apps installed for the agreed period of 14 days. I sincerely hope that other members will also continue to support each other — both inside and outside the group — for the good of the community.

I deeply appreciate all the feedback and support I have received during my time here.

Thank you very much for your understanding.

Warm regards,

Aliwan-dev
