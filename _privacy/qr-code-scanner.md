Thank you for using **QR Code Scanner**. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### 1. Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. We may collect limited non-personal information like device type and crash logs to improve app performance.

### 2. Permissions
- **Camera:** Used only for scanning. Camera input is processed locally and never stored.
- **Storage (optional):** Used to store scan history locally on your device.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Our app may use Google Play Services and AdMob for functionality and non-intrusive advertising. Please review their [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details.
